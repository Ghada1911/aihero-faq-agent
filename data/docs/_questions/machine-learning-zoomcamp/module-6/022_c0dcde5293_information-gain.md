Information gain in Y due to X, or the mutual information of Y and X:

Where \( I(Y; X) = H(Y) - H(Y | X) \).

- If X is completely uninformative about Y, then \( I(Y; X) = 0 \).
- If X is completely informative about Y, then \( I(Y; X) = H(Y) \).
